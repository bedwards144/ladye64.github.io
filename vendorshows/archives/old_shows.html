"Lunches for Learning provides a hot lunch to 75 students every week, at Lansdowne and Queen Elizabeth. Older students help prepare fresh fruits & vegetables. They assist the younger students to get their meal. Everyone sits down to share the meal - students, staff, & volunteers. The program is inclusive, intergenerational, & making a community within the school."
